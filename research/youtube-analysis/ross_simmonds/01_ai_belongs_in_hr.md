## Description

Most companies treat AI as a technology tool, but Ross Simmonds argues that organizations should think of AI as part of a human and non-human workforce. This perspective encourages businesses to redesign workflows rather than simply automate isolated tasks.

---

## Key Topics

- Artificial Intelligence
- Human + AI collaboration
- Business transformation
- Future of work
- Organizational strategy

---

## Research Notes

This video introduces a strategic view of AI adoption. Rather than replacing employees, AI is presented as a workforce participant that can augment human capabilities and improve operational efficiency.

---

## Potential Playbook Insights

- AI should be integrated into business workflows rather than used as a standalone tool.
- Organizations should redefine roles around collaboration between people and AI systems.
- Successful AI adoption requires changes in processes, not only technology implementation.
