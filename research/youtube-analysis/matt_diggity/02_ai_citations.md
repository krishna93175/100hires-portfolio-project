## Research Notes

The presentation explains that AI systems tend to reference content from authoritative and trustworthy sources. Improving content credibility can increase the likelihood of being cited in AI-generated answers.

## Potential Playbook Insights

- Build domain authority consistently.
- Publish accurate and well-supported content.
- Strengthen trust signals across the website.
